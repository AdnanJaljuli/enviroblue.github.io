Equipment & Products

Comprehensive water and wastewater treatment solutions.

We supply a complete range of equipment and chemicals for municipal and industrial treatment facilities.

Chemicals for Wastewater Treatment: Nano Products

  • Improves sludge settling velocity (overloaded plants)
  • Produces compact and stable flocs
  • Improves treatment efficiency in aeration tank by increasing the sludge circulation
  • Reduces smells in the plant by combining with HS
  • Eliminates settling tank problems due to sludge bulking
  • Improves oxygen transfer to bacteria: Energy saving
  • Improves effluent quality by capturing micro particles in the effluent
  • Reduces polymer consumption in sludge dewatering
