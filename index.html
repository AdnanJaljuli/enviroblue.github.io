Equipment & Products

Comprehensive water and wastewater treatment solutions.

We supply a complete range of equipment and chemicals for municipal and industrial treatment facilities.

Chemicals for Wastewater Treatment: Nano Products

Nano-additives that boost settling, efficiency, and odor control.

  • Improves sludge settling velocity and produces compact, stable flocs.
  • Enhances aeration tank performance by improving sludge circulation and oxygen transfer.
  • Reduces odors by combining with H₂S and lowers polymer consumption in dewatering.

Sludge Anaerobic Digester Equipment

Complete biogas handling from digestion to cleaning, storage, and flaring.

  • Digester components: gas hoods, foam traps, pressure security valves, and access openings.
  • Biogas cleaning for condensate, particles, desulphurization, and siloxane removal.
  • Biogas storage and flares: double-membrane/silo-type holders, open/closed, low/high temperature units.

Pumping

Reliable pumping solutions for sludge and wastewater transfer.

  • Progressive cavity pumps for thick sludge and difficult fluids.
  • Peristaltic pumps for precise, low-shear dosing and transfer.
  • Sewage grinders to protect downstream equipment.

Disinfection

UV and chemical systems to achieve safe, compliant effluent.

  • UV systems for open channel installations and closed vessels.
  • Ozone generators for powerful oxidation of pathogens and organics.
  • Hypochlorite oxidant generators for on-site chemical production.

Odor Control Systems

Biological and chemical solutions for effective odor management.

  • Biological systems: bio-filter beds, bio-scrubbers, and bio-trickling filters.
  • Chemical scrubbers for high-load or variable odor streams.
  • Activated carbon polishing for sensitive locations.

Sludge Thickening & Dewatering

Equipment to reduce sludge volume and optimize disposal costs.

  • Gravity belt thickeners and decanter centrifuges for primary thickening.
  • Belt filter presses and screw presses for efficient mechanical dewatering.

Filtration

Robust filtration for pretreatment and final polishing.

  • Sand filter underdrains and rotary disc filters (mesh/pile cloth).
  • Drum filters for pretreatment and cartridge filters for desalination pretreatment.

Mixing Systems

Mixers tailored for equalization, aeration, and chemical mixing.

  • Vertical mixers for water/sewage tanks and sludge digesters.
  • Submersible mixers for equalization and aeration tanks.
  • Static mixers for polymer sludge mixing and chemical dosing.

Vessels and Tank Covers

Pressure vessels and covers that protect assets and the environment.

  • Surge vessels (bladder and dip tube types) for pressure protection.
  • GRP tank covers for odor containment in WWTPs.
  • Floating hexagonal pond covers to reduce evaporation and algae growth.

Reactor Media

High-performance media for biological treatment reactors.

  • Fixed media including MBR and fixed film for trickling filters.
  • Moving bed biofilm reactor (MBBR) media for compact, efficient systems.

Clarifiers and Settling

Clarification systems to improve solids removal.

  • Circular clarifier bridges (half/full, flat/hopper bottom).
  • Chain scrapers for rectangular settling tanks with non-metallic chains.
  • Lamella and tube settlers to increase settling performance.

Head Works

Front-end screening and grit removal for plant protection.

  • Coarse and fine screens plus grit removal systems and classifiers.
  • Gates and penstocks (welded and cast iron) for isolation and flow control.
  • Screw and belt conveyors for screenings and grit handling.

Aeration System

Complete aeration packages for biological treatment.

  • Air blowers: roots, side channel, centrifugal, and turbo types.
  • Surface aerators (high/low speed, fixed/floating) and aeration rotors.
  • Fine and coarse bubble diffusers (disc, tubular, and flat).
